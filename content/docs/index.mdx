## Introduction to PentOpsVault 

### My Personal Stash of Pentesting and Sysadmin Notes

Welcome to my personal stash of pentesting and sysadmin notes! This project is a labor of love, meticulously crafted to share insights, personal notes, and research findings in the realms of Pentesting, Networks, and Sysadmin. As I continue to enrich this repository, it will shine over time, becoming a comprehensive knowledge base for anyone interested in these fields.

### Purpose and Vision

The primary objective of this project is to create a repository of notes that is easily accessible and portable. Whether I'm at home, in the office, or on the go, having my notes readily available ensures that I can continue learning and growing in my understanding of pentesting, networks, and sysadmin tasks.
